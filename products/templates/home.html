Andhra Pradesh is known for its bold flavours and rich culinary traditions. Every meal from this land is enhanced with aromatic chutneys, flavour-packed pickles, traditional spice powders, and mouth-watering sweet delicacies.

Our Andhra pickles stand out for their fiery taste and authentic preparation. Customer favourites include the tangy Avakaya (mango pickle), the iconic Gongura pickle, and the flavour-rich Allam Pachadi (ginger pickle). These classic pickles pair perfectly with any meal, any time.

Andhra is equally celebrated for its traditional sweets like Putharekulu, Ariselu, Bobbatlu, Sunnundalu, and Kajikaya. Prepared during festivals and special occasions, these sweets bring the true essence of Andhra to homes across the country.
